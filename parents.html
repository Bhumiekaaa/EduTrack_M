What is EduTrack?

A secure portal that gives families real-time visibility into attendance, grades, announcements, and upcoming tasks.

Why EduTrack?

  • See progress early and intervene faster
  • Receive instant notifications from teachers and the school
  • Simple, accessible design on any device

Why it's different and secure

  • Granular permissions—access only to your own child(ren)
  • End-to-end encryption in transit and strong encryption at rest
  • Security-first engineering and continuous monitoring

What EduTrack offers parents

  • Attendance alerts and absence summaries
  • Grade reports and term-wise insights
  • Announcements, messages, and calendar events
  • Multi-student linking under one account

About EduTrack

EduTrack is a smart Student Information Management System that simplifies and automates academic and administrative tasks in colleges. It offers a unified platform for students, teachers, administrators, and guardians to efficiently manage and track academic data in real time. By maintaining attendance, grades, and progress reports digitally, EduTrack enhances transparency, accuracy, and communication within institutions. Built with modern technologies, it ensures ease of use, data security, and scalability, supporting colleges in their journey toward digital transformation.

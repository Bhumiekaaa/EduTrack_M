About EduTrack

EduTrack is an innovative Student Information Management System designed to simplify and automate academic and administrative tasks in colleges. It provides a unified digital platform where students, teachers, administrators, and guardians can efficiently manage, access, and track academic information in real time. The system aims to enhance transparency, accuracy, and communication between institutions and stakeholders. From maintaining attendance and grades to sharing progress reports and notices, EduTrack ensures a smooth, organized, and data-driven educational experience. Built with modern web technologies, EduTrack combines ease of use, data security, and scalability, making it a reliable choice for educational institutions aiming for digital transformation.

What is EduTrack?

EduTrack is a modern student information system that centralizes courses, grades, attendance, schedules, and communication in one simple, secure platform.

Why EduTrack?

  • Real-time updates and reminders
  • Clear progress tracking and GPA insights
  • Fast, mobile-friendly interface

Why it's different and secure

  • AES‑256 data encryption and TLS 1.3 in transit
  • Role-based access so only you and approved staff can view your data
  • Regular security audits and least-privilege policies

What EduTrack offers students

  • Personal timetable and reminders
  • Attendance view and absence insights
  • Assignments, submissions, and grades dashboard
  • Semester tracking and CGPA calculator
